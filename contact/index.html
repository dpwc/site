Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I book my first session?

Simply fill out the booking form above with your details and preferred service. Our team will reach out within 24 hours to confirm your appointment slot.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes, we offer secure video-call sessions for clients who prefer remote counseling or are unable to visit in person. You can select your preferred mode in the booking form.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. All client information is handled with strict confidentiality in accordance with professional ethical guidelines. Your data is never shared without explicit consent.

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session is an initial consultation where your counselor will listen to your concerns, understand your goals, and collaboratively outline a plan tailored to your needs.

What time can I contact you?

You can contact us anytime through phone, email, or our online contact form. If we're unavailable at the moment, we'll get back to you as soon as possible to assist you with your inquiry.

Ready to take the first step?

You don't have to have it all figured out. Just reach out — we'll walk the rest together.
