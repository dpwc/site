About the Centre

Healing is not just a service; it is a mission of the heart.

De Paul Wellness Centre is a compassionate Vincentian initiative, rooted in the timeless values of Saint Vincent de Paul: charity, humility, and service to the most vulnerable.

We believe true health is not merely the absence of illness, but the flourishing of the whole person in body, mind, and soul. Inspired by the Vincentian spirit of practical love and human dignity, we offer a welcoming space where every person regardless of background or circumstance is seen, heard, and cared for with warmth and respect.

Our Vision

To create a sanctuary of healing and growth where individuals, families, and youth discover inner peace, resilience, and holistic well-being through professional psychological support rooted in Vincentian compassion.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, professional psychotherapy and counselling that addresses the emotional, psychological, and spiritual needs of people in their daily struggles — fostering mental health awareness and resilience within the Aluva community.

Our Founder

Fr. Dr. Shijo Antony CM

Founder · De Paul Centre for Holistic Wellness & Mindfulness

Hailing from Kannur, Kerala, Fr. Dr. Shijo was ordained a Vincentian priest in 2004. He holds a Doctorate in Canon Law from Spain and a Doctorate in Positive Psychology from the USA, and is a Diplomate of Positive Psychology from the American Board of Positive Psychology.

His ministry has taken him across more than 40 countries and diverse cultures, enriching his understanding of the human experience. A mindfulness practitioner, motivational speaker, therapist, and author, he trains educators, youth, and professionals to live meaningful, fulfilled lives.
