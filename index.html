About the Centre

Healing is not just a service; it is a mission of the heart.

We believe true health is not merely the absence of illness, but the flourishing of the whole person in body, mind, and soul. Inspired by the Vincentian spirit of practical love and human dignity, we offer a welcoming space where every person regardless of background or circumstance is seen, heard, and cared for with respect.

Our Vision

To create a sanctuary of healing and growth where individuals, families, and youth discover inner peace, resilience, and holistic wellness

Our Mission

To provide accessible, professional psychotherapy and counselling that addresses the emotional, needs of people.

Founder · De Paul Centre for Holistic Wellness & Mindfulness
